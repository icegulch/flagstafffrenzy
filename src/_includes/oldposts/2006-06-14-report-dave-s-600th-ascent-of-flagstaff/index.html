Report: Dave's 600th Ascent of Flagstaff

June 14, 2006

Dave makes it 600! (gallery)

Thanks to everyone for participating in today's momentous ride, especially our surprise refreshments goddess, Kristen!!! Congratulations to Dave for making it to #600, to Tom for making it to #1, and to everyone else who's somewhere in-between!

Dave still claims he likes the "new" bike, but he still despises the heat...100°F today!! If you'd like to relive the climb from the comfort of your air-conditioned office, check out the gallery. Otherwise, see you again soon in the hills!
