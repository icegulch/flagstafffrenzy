Flagstaff Frenzy

Boxing Boulder Ride Report

September 18, 2007

After getting ditched by everyone, Matt and I decided to forgo the planned ride and do Boxing Boulder instead.

Matt has kindly recorded and published all our vital signs from the ride, using his snazzy GPS "watch". As you can see, despite the nagging winds and momentum-busting stoplights, we hemmed in our favorite town rather swiftly.
