Northwest Passage Report

April 15, 2010

Some call it the iPaw

The Coast Guard had it spot on: there were open channels rendering the Northwest Passage navigable. Thanks to a sextant and Rob's keen eyes, we made it back to campus safely.

The passage was not without peril, though. Indeed, the moondust that builds up in the hairpin corners tugged at our front wheels on the ascent. We usually don't see said dust until July!
