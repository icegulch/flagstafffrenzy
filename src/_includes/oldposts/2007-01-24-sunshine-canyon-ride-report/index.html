Sunshine Canyon Ride Report

January 24, 2007

This is what people seem to think of my ideas

Wasn't such a dumb idea to go out today! Sunshine Canyon was largely dry, except for one or two spots in the hollow at the bottom of the canyon. Otherwise, smooth sailing—almost like summer.

Still, attendance continues to be down. Given the number of lurkers on this site, we should be seeing more folks! According to Google Analytics, there are 379 visitors, 167 new ones! Where are you?!
