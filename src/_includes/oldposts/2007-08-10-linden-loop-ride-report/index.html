Linden Loop Ride Report

August 10, 2007

View Linden Loop in a larger map

Though we did a little Lindy Hopping beforehand, we were all business when riding Linden Loop for the first time in a while today.

Dave crunched the numbers and deemed the ride "safe" for a coumadin patient—I think he uses an old Russian calculator. I'd have to say, he's looking pretty healthy to me.

Mr. U flew up Linden like a Kazakh on Team Astana, making it tough for drug-free Matt to latch on.
